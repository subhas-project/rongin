Rongin Detergent Powder

Rongin Detergent Powder is perfect for removing tough stains with ease while keeping clothes fresh and soft.

  • Removes tough stains effortlessly.
  • Provides a long-lasting fresh fragrance.
  • Gentle on fabrics, tough on dirt.
  • Available in sizes: 130g, 400g, and 800g.

King Dishwash Bar is the ultimate solution for removing grease and oil from utensils effectively.

  • Gentle on hands, ensuring smooth skin.
